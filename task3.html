Boxing is a widely followed combat sport that emphasizes strength, agility, and strategy. With origins dating back centuries, modern boxing became organized and popularized in the 20th century. It features high-intensity bouts between fighters who use skillful footwork, powerful punches, and defensive techniques to outmatch their opponents. The sport has evolved with global competitions, including the Olympics and world championships. With intense training, iconic rivalries, and passionate fanbases, boxing remains one of the most recognized and thrilling sports worldwide.
