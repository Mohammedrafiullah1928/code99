About the Movie

Vada Chennai is a gripping Tamil gangster film that chronicles three decades of gang wars in North Chennai. The story follows Anbu, a skilled carrom player who reluctantly becomes involved in the criminal underworld. Through non-linear storytelling, the film explores themes of loyalty, revenge, and survival in one of Chennai's most notorious neighborhoods. With powerful performances and realistic portrayal of gang politics, the movie became a critical and commercial success.

Director: Vetrimaaran
Release Year: 2018
Genre: Crime, Drama, Action
Duration: 164 minutes
