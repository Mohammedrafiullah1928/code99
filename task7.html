nadhaswaram

Nadhaswaram is a popular Tamil television serial directed by Thirumurugan, known for his debut serial Metti Oli. The serial, which focuses on family-oriented themes, was primarily filmed in Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu, and featured many debutant actors. Thirumurugan also directed the show's 1000th episode as a live telecast, a feat that earned it a place in the Guinness World Records.
