Our Story

The journey of Velvethrift

Where It All Began

Velvethrift was born from a deep appreciation for the elegance and craftsmanship of bygone eras. In a world of fast fashion and disposable trends, we saw an opportunity to celebrate the enduring beauty of vintage clothing and luxury thrift pieces.

Our founder, inspired by old-money aesthetics and the timeless style of classic cinema, began curating a collection that would offer others the chance to own pieces with history, character, and unmatched quality.

Our Philosophy

At Velvethrift, we believe that style should be sustainable, sophisticated, and personal. Each garment we select tells a story and represents a moment in fashion history worth preserving.

We are committed to authenticity, quality, and providing our customers with pieces that transcend seasonal trends. Whether it's a 1960s silk dress or a vintage Chanel bag, every item is carefully inspected and restored to ensure it meets our exacting standards.

What Sets Us Apart

Unlike typical thrift stores, Velvethrift specializes in high-end vintage and designer pieces. We source our collection from estate sales, private collectors, and exclusive vintage markets around the world.

Our curation process is meticulous. We look for timeless silhouettes, premium fabrics, and impeccable construction. The result is a boutique experience that combines the thrill of thrifting with the luxury of designer shopping.

Our Values

Sustainability

We believe in giving beautiful garments a second life and reducing fashion waste

Authenticity

Every piece is verified for authenticity and quality before joining our collection

Timeless Style

We curate pieces that will never go out of fashion and can be worn for years to come
