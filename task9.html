Tamil Serials

Nadhaswaram

Nadhaswaram is a Tamil television drama that aired from 2010 to 2015 on Sun TV. Known for its realistic storytelling and record-breaking continuous shots, it revolves around Gopi and his extended family, highlighting values, traditions, and interpersonal relationships in a rural setting.

Kalyanaveedu

Kalyanaveedu is a family drama that aired from 2015 to 2018 on Sun TV. The series focuses on the life of Gomathi and Gnanam, exploring the complexities of joint family relationships, generational gaps, and the importance of tradition amidst changing times.

Metti Oli

Metti Oli is a classic Tamil serial aired from 2002 to 2005 on Sun TV. It tells the story of Chidambaram and his five daughters, portraying their struggles, love lives, and how they uphold family honor. The serial is remembered for its emotional storytelling and relatable characters.

Chitti

Chitti is a Tamil drama series that aired from 1999 to 2001 on Sun TV. Starring Radhika Sarathkumar, it revolves around the character of Ranjini, a stepmother who faces family challenges and societal judgments while trying to unite her family with love and care.
